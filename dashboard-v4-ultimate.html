FAQ

How to connect?
Download the app for your device, copy the subscription link, and import it into the app.
Connection issues?
Try switching servers. If problem persists, update the subscription and restart the app.
How to renew?
Transfer the amount to the card number above and send the receipt via Telegram.
Why is the speed slow?
Try connecting to a different server closer to your location. Check your internet connection and ensure no other apps are consuming bandwidth.
How to change servers?
In your VPN app, tap on the server list and select a different server. We recommend choosing servers with green status for best performance.
Can I use on multiple devices?
Yes! You can use your subscription on multiple devices simultaneously. Simply import the same config on each device.
How to cancel subscription?
Contact our support team via Telegram @rahbarusd. Your subscription will remain active until the expiry date.
Are activity logs kept?
No. We have a strict no-logs policy. Your browsing activity and connection logs are never stored.
What protocols are supported?
We support VLESS, VMess, Trojan, and Shadowsocks protocols with TLS and Reality encryption for maximum security.
How to fix DNS issues?
In your app settings, try changing DNS to 1.1.1.1 or 8.8.8.8. Also ensure DNS routing is enabled in the app.
Can I get a static IP?
Static IP is available as a premium add-on. Contact support @rahbarusd for pricing and availability.
What if I run out of data?
